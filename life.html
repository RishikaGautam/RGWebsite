ORIGIN STORY

I grew up between two worlds — the warmth of family and the cold glow of a computer screen I kept sneaking back to. The first time code I wrote actually did something, I felt like I'd discovered a superpower hidden in plain sight.

I'm the kind of person who asks "why does this work?" before "how do I make it work." Math was my first love — it was honest, it didn't care about your feelings, only whether you were right.

NYU brought me to New York City, which turned out to be exactly the kind of chaos I needed. There's something about a city that never stops that makes you want to build things that matter.
